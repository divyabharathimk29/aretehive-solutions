FAQs

How will you understand my business?

We will understand your business before starting any social media management for your business. We will send you a questionnaire on completion of which a kick-off call shall be scheduled with you to discuss the answered questionnaire. After the kickoff call, we will devise a social media strategy sewn around your business requirements and industry trends.

Who will be working on my account?

You will be assigned a dedicated social media manager who is trained in social media marketing. This social media manager will be your one point of contact for all the social media requirements of your business.

What about content?

No, you do not have to provide us with content. The dedicated social media manager assigned to you will develop unique content that fits your brand with the help of our team of content/copywriters and graphic designers. We have access to stock photography, resources like design software, as well as relevant news sources. We develop engaging content for your target audience using these resources.our online presence is what helps businesses grow by having a high visibility among potential customers. Any business, big or small, should have the online presence expansion as a key part of their.

How my social media followers will grow?

We grow social media followers using both; organic as well as the paid approach. Deploying the two approaches depend on various factors such as need and budget.

What about social media account management?

We will take care of your social accounts across the social media platforms. Right from monitoring posts or campaigns for comments, messages, and reviews to answering and interacting with your potential customers.

What results can I expect?

The key performance indicators we look at are the number of followers, engagement rate, and the traffic to your website. These 3 metrics influence sales in the long-term. You will see these metrics increasing monthly, and we will track the progress to see how this is working for you.

Is there any setup Fees?

There is a one-time setup fee and it is primarily for getting you set up at our end to run campaigns. We set you up in our search engine systems, analytics systems, advertising accounts, and other administrative tasks.

How much website traffic should I expect every month?

Your website traffic is directly related to the number of new followers you gain. Both paid as well as organic approach builds followers. However, to see an immediate increase in website traffic, you should opt for paid advertising.

How soon I can expect to see the results?

In terms of the number of followers and engagement rate usually, the change is visible within the first 30 days of operation. The results should continue to increase month on month. We recommend our clients to be on the network for at least 6-12 months to see a noticeable impact. However, if you want to focus on driving conversions in the short-term, you should consider using paid advertising.

How long are the contracts?

We offer initial contract terms of 3 months, after which your account will be serviced on a month-to-month basis. You have an option to cancel at any time with a 30 day written or digital notice, after which we cancel all services.

How long does it take to get started?

If you are ready to get started, you are just a click away.
